About AquaLease.

A Nigerian water infrastructure company, on a mission to make safe water affordable for every household.

Founded in 2022 in Lagos, AquaLease combines consumer-friendly instalment plans with industrial-grade engineering. Our customers are households, schools, churches, and small businesses — anyone who wants safe water without the upfront cost.

Today we serve 36 cities, hold a 98.6% on-time payment rate, and have placed 12,400+ units into service. Every device we ship is matched to local water conditions, and every customer gets a dedicated WhatsApp support line.
